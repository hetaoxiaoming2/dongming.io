☆ Home service robot based on cam linkage mechanism

Posted: 2020-11-20   |   Categories: Competition   |   Tags: Cam-linkage Mechanism, Competition, First Prize, Mechanical innovation

* Copetition Introduction

  • The 9th National College Students Mechanical Innovation Design Competition:
  •                         __"Smart Home, Happy Family"__
  1. Mechanical devices to help the elderly live alone (referred to as machinery for helping the elderly);
  2. Mechanical devices of modern smart home (referred to as smart home machinery) for “machinery to assist the elderly “, The design focuses on assisting the elderly to sit up, get up and down from bed, go to the toilet and take a bath when they are alone at home. Prevent the fall injury, assist the fall after standing; A mechanical device that reminds you to take medicine, organize and access things easily. It also includes a mechanical device designed to help the elderly go up and down the stairs for families living in duplex floors.
  • Project Selection: Mechanical devices to help the elderly live alone

          “Home service robot based on cam linkage mechanism”

     In this project, as a participating member, I was mainly responsible for the processing of the mechanical structure and the electrical control of the motor, and integrated a wireless video module based on the OpenWrt system for video transmission in the robot. And we also won the honor of national first prize, which was reported by many news media. And I became the first student in the history of Chongqing University to win two first prizes in the national competition of the same machine innovation competition.

  • 第九届全国大学生机械创新设计大赛:
  •                         __“智慧家居、幸福家庭”__
  1. 帮助老年人独自活动起居的机械装置（简称助老机械）；
  2. 现代智能家居的机械装置（简称智能家居机械）对于"助老机械”, 重点设计当老人独自在家活动时,辅助其从床上坐立、上下床、如厕、洗浴;预防其跌伤、辅助其跌倒后站立;提醒吃药、物品整理和方便存取等方面的机械装置。还包括针对居住复式楼层家庭，设计帮助老人上下楼的机械装置。
