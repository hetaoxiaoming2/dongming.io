☆ Gravity potential energy trolley based on cam mechanism

Posted: 2019-06-01   |   Categories: Competition   |   Tags: Engineering Ability Training Competition, Second prize

* Competition Introduction

  • The 6th National College Student Engineering Training Comprehensive Ability Competition
  • 第六届全国大学生工程训练综合能力竞赛

     This competition is organized by the Chinese Ministry of Education, which was renamed as China College Students Engineering Time and Innovation Ability Competition in 2021. The competition level is second only to the National College Students Mechanical Innovation Design Competition. Relevant competition documentation can be downloaded by clicking here

  • Topic Selection: A bicycle with directional control function driven by gravitational potential energy

    Our competition topic is “A bicycle with directional control function driven by gravitational potential energy”. Independently design and produce a self-propelled car with directional control function, requiring that the energy required to complete all movements during its walking process be converted from the given gravitational potential energy, and no other source of energy can be used. The given gravitational potential energy is uniformly used as a standard weight with a mass of 1Kg during the competition（ Φ fifty × 65 mm, made of carbon steel ）, with a requirement that the weight can be lowered to a height of 400 ± 2mm. The standard weight is always carried by the trolley.

    Our group innovatively used the gear rack mechanism composite cam mechanism for the first time in this competition. Replacing the traditional cam mechanism steering scheme. Compared to traditional schemes, our scheme greatly reduces the curvature radius of the cam. At the same time, it also makes the car design more compact and compact. Higher energy utilization rate. Due to inadequate consideration of ground friction, a certain mistake occurred at the national competition site. But even so, we still won the national second prize. Especially, at that time, all members of our team were only sophomores.

    In the above video, you can see the scene where I am processing parts and debugging the car. In this project, I am mainly responsible for the design of the model car and the processing and assembly of components. Through this competition, I was able to proficiently master skills such as 3D printing, laser cutting, wire cutting, and even possess the ability of CNC milling machines.
