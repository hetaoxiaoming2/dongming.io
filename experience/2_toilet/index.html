☆ Toilet Assist Device Based on Cam Linkage Mechanism

Posted: 2020-11-25   |   Categories: Competition   |   Tags: Cam-linkage Mechanism, Competition, First Prize, Mechanical innovation

* Copetition Introduction

  • The 9th National College Students Mechanical Innovation Design Competition:
  •                         __"Smart Home, Happy Family"__
  1. Mechanical devices to help the elderly live alone (referred to as machinery for helping the elderly);
  2. Mechanical devices of modern smart home (referred to as smart home machinery) for “machinery to assist the elderly “, The design focuses on assisting the elderly to sit up, get up and down from bed, go to the toilet and take a bath when they are alone at home. Prevent the fall injury, assist the fall after standing; A mechanical device that reminds you to take medicine, organize and access things easily. It also includes a mechanical device designed to help the elderly go up and down the stairs for families living in duplex floors.
  • Project Selection: Mechanical devices to help the elderly live alone

     In this project, as the team leader, I am mainly responsible for the construction of team, leading topic selection, mechanical structure model design, and physical processes. After six months of topic selection and mechanism selection, we have finally decided to use a cam linkage mechanism to design a power assisted toilet device that can achieve cellular function. And we won the honor of national first prize, which was reported by many news media..

  • Here are our design details:

    First, we extract the human sitting and standing trajectory through the Tracker software, so as to obtain the human squatting trajectory equation by fitting.

     Afterwards, according to the splitting principle of the mechanism rod group, the mechanism function is divided into three rod groups for design, namely, the position control rod group, the attitude control rod group and the cam point control rod. According to the geometric relationship, the MATLAB mathematical model is constructed and under the constraint of realizing the clamshell function of the seat cushion, the genetic algorithm (GA) is used for optimization to obtain the mechanism rod group and cam parameters. In this paper, the force analysis of the bar group is carried out according to the mathematical model, and the strength of the bar is estimated based on the result of the force analysis, and then the three-dimensional modeling is carried out in SolidWorks.

    For further model and structural verification, this paper uses ADAMS for kinematics analysis, and SolidWorks Motion for dynamics and rough member finite element analysis. Based on the above software simulation results, this paper selects key members and key node bolts, and uses ANSYS finite element analysis to perform strength analysis and check. The rationality and feasibility of the design are verified.

    The team finally scaled down the design by 1:2, and processed metal parts to make a physical prototype for functional demonstration, which can achieve the design goal. After the auxiliary electronic control related equipment is installed, the design device can realize the voice-controlled smooth lifting and assisting process and the flip function.
