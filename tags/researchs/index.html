☆☆ Variable admittance control for puncture robot

Posted: 2023-05-03   |   Categories: Researchs   |   Tags: Machine Creation, Medical Robot, Researchs

In the process of teleoperation minimally invasive puncture microwave ablation surgery, due to the regular respiratory ups and downs of the human chest during the operation, the end of the ablation puncture robot is usually rigid in the past, which is likely to cause human tissue tearing near the rigid needle. In order to reduce and avoid the secondary damage caused by the relative movement between the puncture needle and the thoracic skin to the surrounding living tissue, we aim at the breathing movement of the patient in the puncture ablation operation, and based on the six-dimensional force sensor and fuzzy control, we have established a breathing-following system.
